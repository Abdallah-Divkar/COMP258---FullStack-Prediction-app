Model Architecture

Framework: FastAPI + scikit-learn
Classifier: MLPClassifier
Hidden layers: (64, 32)
Activation: ReLU
Optimizer: Adam
Preprocessing:
- Median imputation for numeric fields
- Most-frequent imputation for categorical fields
- StandardScaler for numeric fields
- OneHotEncoder for categorical fields
